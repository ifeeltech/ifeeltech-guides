## How We Use Affiliate Links

### Product Recommendations

We only recommend products and services that:

- We have direct experience with, or
- Are widely recognized as quality solutions in the IT industry, or
- Are relevant to the topic being discussed

**We do not recommend products solely because they offer affiliate commissions.**

### Transparency

Affiliate links in our articles are marked with special URLs (e.g., `ifeeltech.com/go-product-name`). These redirect links:

- Make it clear when you're using an affiliate link
- Allow us to track which recommendations are helpful
- Help us maintain and improve our content

### Editorial Independence

Our affiliate relationships **do not influence** our editorial content. We:

- Provide honest assessments of products
- Acknowledge limitations and trade-offs
- Compare multiple options (including non-affiliate products)
- Focus on helping you make informed decisions

## Your Choice

Using our affiliate links is entirely optional. You can:

- Use our links to support our work (at no extra cost to you)
- Go directly to the retailer's website
- Search for products independently

**Either way, you'll pay the same price.**
